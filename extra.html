Update

Now, the following is an update on me. This was written not long after it occurred so you're getting something fairly new. As mentioned above, it has to do with something we had spoken about before. I thought to give you a heads up before throughing you into the Update. But now that you know this, you may continue.

A couple things have happened since the last time we've spoken. The main one being I got a hair cut. That might not be big news or anything but to me it is. As you know, I had wanted to cut my hair. Actually, I don't even know if that is what I told you. I think I just said I didn't trust my mom cutting my hair anymore. Anyways, I had told my sister, Edith, and our friend, Susan, if they would be down to go to the nail salon to get my hair cut. This nail salon is where they both get there nails done and where they happen to cut hair as well. Since it was a place that was familiar, I was willing to go there to get a hair cut. I had pitched we should go there soon but we never set a date to go. Fast-forward a week later, my sister shows up unexpectedly at my home and tells me if I am ready to go to the nail salon. I was caught off guard as by that time I had decided to grow out my hair. As you could imagine, I was struggling to make a decision. It was now or never my sister said. After a minute of deep thinking, I said fuck it and off we went. My older sister, Mariana, ended up tagging along as well. So we had the whole gang there: both of my sisters, Susan (our friend) and Nadia (Susan's daughter).

I was nervous, of course, as I had not gotten my hair cut by someone other than my mom or away from home before. I was also scared that they would mess up the hair cut. Which, if you remember, I had said I have had my hair cut bad before and that I didn't mind getting a bad hair cut. Well, I was paying money this time around so my hair cut needed to come out good😭😂 No, I'm playing. Good or bad hair cut, I was going for the experience. If I didn't like the hair cut I'd just grow it out.

So how did it go? Well, the first half of the hair cut was a breeze. I was excited after all. It wasn't until she kept changing guards that I began to get nervous again. At some point, she took out an electric razor that had a sharp blade and let me tell you she was going at it with it. She was doing things I have never experienced before when getting a hair cut so I was panicking a bit. But I told myself to trust the process. That she knows what she's doing. Fast-forward to the end of the hair cut, I'm glad I trusted the process because I loved how it turned out.

Seeing myself in the mirror was a trip. I was like damn is that me?😂 Like for the first time I actually got the hair cut I had asked for😂 Moving away from that, when everyone got the full hair cut reveal they treated it as if I had won the lottery or something😂 They were all in shock and complimenting me on how good it looked. Even one of the workers chimed in and said I looked good and that short hair suited me. I was honestly happy that everyone shared the same joy I did. That we all liked the hair cut. So as you can see I had a great positive experience and as you can expect, I will definitely be returning to the nail salon if I want a new hair cut😂

As for the look, I really do look and feel different. Crazy what a hair cut can do to a person.

Anyways, the only person who was not happy with the look was my mom, which was expected. She has always preferred my hair long. I didn't mind her reaction though. I was happy with my hair cut and that's all that matters.
